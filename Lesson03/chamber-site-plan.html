Purpose

The purpose of this site and group is to promote growth and development within the individuals living in or near Provo/Utah County and not just in the community. This will be done through job listings and business ventures that are happening around these areas. The idea behind this is to bring back the jobs and economy to the community. To help get this community running and provide a place for those that still need the support to get their businesses, lives, and families back to normal through jobs, activities, and promotions.
